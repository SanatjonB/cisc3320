1-	Snack Producer and Consumer

Implement Snack Producer and Consumer using the following info:
•	A SnackProducer puts snacks in a shared basket (buffer).
•	A SnackConsumer takes snacks from the basket.
•	Basket can hold 3 snacks at most.
•	Use counting semaphores to manage empty/full slots and a binary semaphore for mutual exclusion.
